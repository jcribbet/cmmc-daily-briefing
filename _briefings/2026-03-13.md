## Regulatory & DoD Updates

### 1. SBA Hosts Small Business Roundtable on CMMC Impacts
**Source:** U.S. SBA Office of Advocacy | March 12, 2026\
[View Article](https://advocacy.sba.gov/2026/02/24/department-of-war-cybersecurity-maturity-model-certification-cmmc-program-small-business-impacts-roundtable/)

The SBA Office of Advocacy held a virtual roundtable on March 12 to collect direct input from small businesses on CMMC compliance costs and program improvement opportunities. Advocacy previously filed comments stating the DoD underestimated compliance costs — this session sought real-world data from contractors now navigating Phase 1.

### 2. CMMC Phase 2 Prep Guide: Mandatory C3PAO Assessments Start Nov 2026
**Source:** Secureframe | March 4, 2026\
[View Article](https://secureframe.com/blog/cmmc-phase-2-preparation)

Comprehensive breakdown of Phase 2 changes. Key stat: 93% of organizations handling CUI will require Level 2 C3PAO certification. Only ~5% may qualify for self-assessment. C3PAOs already reporting full calendars through late 2026 with 8-12 week assessment timelines.

### 3. GSA Issues CMMC Primer for Contract Holders
**Source:** U.S. General Services Administration | Feb 12, 2026\
[View Article](https://www.gsa.gov/blog/2026/02/12/get-to-know-the-cybersecurity-maturity-model-certification)

GSA reminds its contract holders that CMMC certification is mandatory for DoD contracting opportunities. Outlines all three CMMC levels and urges contractors to understand their FCI/CUI data flows.

### 4. DoD CIO: Submit Affirmations with CMMC Assessments in SPRS
**Source:** DoD CIO\
[View Article](https://dodcio.defense.gov/cmmc/About/)

The DoD CIO CMMC page now prominently features a reminder that affirmations must accompany CMMC assessments in SPRS. Phase 1 remains focused on Level 1 and Level 2 self-assessments, but contracting officers retain discretion to require C3PAO certification.
